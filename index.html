Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Innovation is the process of creating new ideas, methods, or products that bring improvement, efficiency, and transformative change.

Sustainability

Sustainability is the practice of meeting present needs while preserving resources, minimizing environmental impact, and ensuring future generations' well-being.

Infrastructure

Infrastructure comprises essential facilities, systems, and structures like roads, bridges, and utilities, enabling economic growth, connectivity, and societal development.

Empowerment

Empowerment is the process of giving individuals or communities the tools, confidence to make decisions and drive change.
